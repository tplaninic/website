CROATIAN LABOUR LAW

Records that pass inspection

Since October 2024 every Croatian employer has to keep detailed work-time records under the new Pravilnik, NN 55/24. Start and end of work, breaks, overtime, night hours, absences. Wrok keeps all of it as a side effect of the schedule you already run.

Fair questions

Is it really free?+

Yes. Up to 4 workers and 1 workplace, forever, with the full scheduling feature set. No card, no trial timer. When the team grows past that, plans start at €14.99 a month.

Does it cover Croatian work-time record rules?+

Yes. Wrok exports an “Evidencija radnog vremena” workbook built around the NN 55/24 fields: start and end of work, breaks, daily totals, overtime, night and Sunday hours, absence categories. Electronic records are explicitly allowed by the Pravilnik, so this can be your only record system.

What platforms does it run on?+

The web app runs in any browser. On a desktop it’s a full scheduling grid with drag and drop, not a stretched phone screen. The Android app is on Google Play. iOS is on its way.

How does the automatic scheduling actually work?+

It’s a constraint engine, not a magic wand. It takes your shift types, staffing minimums, rest-hour rules, rotations, availability and absences, and searches for a week that satisfies all of them. When that’s impossible it doesn’t guess. It shows which shift failed, why, and what change would fix it.

We run everything through Excel and a group chat. How hard is switching?+

Set up your shift types, invite workers with a code, generate your first week. Maybe fifteen minutes. Keep the old Excel around for reference. Most teams stop opening it after the second week.

Where is my data stored?+

In the EU, over HTTPS, under GDPR. You can export or delete your data whenever you want. Only members of your workplace can see your team’s information.
