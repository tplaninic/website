3 hours back every week

Most owners report cutting weekly scheduling from 2–4 hours down to under 10 minutes. That's a full work day reclaimed every month.

Zero forgotten constraints

Approved vacations, sick days, minimum rest hours, role requirements — the solver respects them all. No more "I forgot Ana was off this week".

Fair workload distribution

Wrok balances hours across the team automatically. Nobody gets stuck with five night shifts in a row while others coast.

Transparent, rule-based scheduling means workers stop blaming you for "unfair" rosters. The schedule is what it is, generated by the same rules every week.
