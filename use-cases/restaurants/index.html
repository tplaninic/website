Restaurant problems Wrok solves

The five scheduling headaches that disappear

Weekend coverage gaps

Set minimum staffing per role per shift. Wrok won't publish a Saturday night with only one server.

Last-minute call-outs

A cook calls in sick at 9 AM — tap once to mark them out, regenerate, push the update. Done before lunch.

Split shifts and double-doubles

Define unlimited custom shifts. Morning + Dinner split? Three Saturday doubles? Configured once, used forever.

Servers swapping nights themselves

Workers negotiate trades through the app. Manager approves with one tap. Zero WhatsApp threads.

Forgetting who's on vacation

Approved time off blocks the scheduler from assigning that worker. No more "wait, wasn't Ana off this week?"

Payroll exports for accountants

PDF or Excel export of weekly hours — including overtime, weekends, and holidays — ready for your accountant.
