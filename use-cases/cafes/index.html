Six things every cafe owner deals with

Student baristas with rotating class schedules

Each worker sets weekly availability. Wrok only schedules them when they're free.

Morning rush coverage

Set "minimum 2 baristas between 7–10 AM". The auto-scheduler refuses to publish a roster that violates it.

Sunday calls from sick baristas

Mark them out, regenerate. Workers who are available get notified about the open Sunday shift instantly.

Split shifts (open + close)

Define an "Open" shift (6:30–11) and a "Close" shift (15–22) separately. Same worker can do both or different ones.

The "I told you I was off" problem

Vacation requests go through the app with a paper trail. No more arguments about who told whom on a Tuesday.

Owner who works the bar too

Schedule yourself in. Wrok treats the boss as a worker for coverage. Common for small cafes where the owner pulls shots.
