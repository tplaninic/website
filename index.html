How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No training needed. Set up once, and let AI do the heavy lifting from day one.

1

Set Up Your Workplace

Create your workplace, invite team members, define shift types with custom times and colors, and configure roles. Everything is ready in under 5 minutes.

  • Define shift types (Morning, Afternoon, Night, or custom)
  • Create roles and set minimum staffing levels
  • Choose working days for each employee
2

Let AI Generate Your Schedule

Hit the generate button and our AI creates an optimized, fair schedule in seconds. It analyzes every constraint simultaneously — or go manual and assign shifts exactly where you want them.

  • AI engine respects all constraints automatically
  • Fine-tune any assignment with a single tap
  • Copy schedules to future weeks in one click
3

Manage & Communicate

Your team sees their schedule instantly. Handle swap requests, track attendance, export reports, and chat — all without leaving the app.

  • Workers get push notifications for schedule updates
  • Approve swaps and time-off requests on the go
  • Export PDF/Excel reports for payroll and compliance
